About Us

Cafe 8848 is a family owned and run business which established in 2016 at Noosa Junction,Noosa Heads. The cafe is open from 7:30 A:M till 5:00 pm,7 days for breakfast and lunch.

Cafe 8848 provide a unique intimate experience to its customers.Cafe serving the best coffee and food to make your day eat in or take away.We also offer catering services for functions at the cafe or at your preferred location.Pick up or free delivery available for larger orders.
